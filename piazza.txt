@265 [Python Aliasing & Copying Lists]

print(a)  # Unchanged: [1, 2, 3, 4]

@269 [What‚Äôs super() in Python?]

@270 [Difference Between Composition & Inheritance]

	‚Ä¢	Inheritance: ‚ÄúIs-a‚Äù relationship (Car is-a Vehicle).
